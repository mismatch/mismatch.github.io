About me

I have an experience in total rewriting and step-by-step refactoring of such solutions. It is very important to keep software and processes around it in a shape. That's why I'm a big proponent of clean code and architecture, DevOps and DX (developer experience). Also I see value in mature technologies and tools that keep evolving. This is a good base for a long-living software. I keep learning new things as modernization happens when you use something modern (let me be your Captain Obvious).
